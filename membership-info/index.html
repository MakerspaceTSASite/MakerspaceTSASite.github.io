Membership Information

A membership card can be purchased in-person during business hours. There are two kinds of memberships, the standard membership and the premium membership. The standard mebership is currently $4 a month, and removes the cost for renting and using equipment. The premium membership is currently $15 a month, and being a premium member gives you the same benefits as the standard membership, as well as allows to you reserve any amount of equipment concurrently, as opposed to the non-member reservation limit of 5 pieces of equipment. Being a premium member also gives you priority over standard members, regarding reserving equipment. If you are not a member, the cost for renting equipment varies depending on what it is, but is generally below $10.
